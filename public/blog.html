The Healing Power of Tallow

Published July 2025

Tallow, rendered from grass-fed beef fat, has been used for centuries as a deeply nourishing balm for the skin. Rich in fat-soluble vitamins A, D, E, and K, it mimics the natural oils of our skin more closely than plant oils. This makes it especially effective at healing dry, cracked, or inflamed skin.

Tallow also contains conjugated linoleic acid (CLA), which has anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It penetrates deeply without clogging pores, making it perfect for sensitive skin, eczema, and even baby care. At Matris Apothecary, our tallow is 100% grass-fed and minimally processed to preserve its healing power.

Gentle Ingredients That Soothe Children’s Skin

Published July 2025

When it comes to children, we choose only the safest and most effective ingredients. Calendula, chamomile, and comfrey are herbs known for their anti-inflammatory and skin-regenerating properties. These herbs help soothe diaper rash, scrapes, bug bites, and more without irritating sensitive skin.

Combined with nourishing carriers like olive oil, shea butter, and beeswax, these botanicals support skin healing naturally. Our products like the Happy Lips & Skin Balm are safe for everyday use—even for little ones. We never use synthetic fragrances, dyes, or preservatives.
